Welcome to S.M.A.R.T.!

Who are we?

We are a group of researchers and educators from the Department of Physics of the University of Chicago.

What is SMART?

The SMART (Science, Mathematics, and Research Training) program is a five-day summer program we have been running at the University of Chicago for nearly 15 years now. Our goal is to share the excitement and secrets of science research with you!

How do you explore this website?

Try the links on the left. If you know any other interesting websites, please tell us. We will include more interesting websites.

How do I apply?

Please fill out and submit the application form below. The program is free of cost!
